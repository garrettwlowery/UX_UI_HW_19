About Me

I am a junior UX/UI designer and researcher with a passion for problem solving and creating enjoyable and memerable expierences for users. Utilizing my background in ethnographic research that I gained while obttaining my degree in anthropology, as well as my UX/UI skills,I am able to empathize with the user and create impactful design solutions that improve the lives of others.

My UX Skills

USER RESEARCH

User research should define the problems that the user is having with a product and determine possible solutions. Methods such as user interviews, competitor analysis, and surveys prove to be extremely helpful during this process.

USER EMPATHY

User empathy is important for understanding the emotions that a user might go through when using a product such as frustration due to error messages or joy when something operates smoothly. Tools such as empathy maps and user journeys are helpful in this process.

QUALITATIVE RESEARCH

I approach qualitative research with an ethnographic view. Methods such as participant observation as well as face-to-face interviews allow me to better understand subjective matters about the user as well as how they use the product and what challenges they might have.
